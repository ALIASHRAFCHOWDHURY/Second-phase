  • Circuit (3 rounds)
  • Push-ups (10-15 reps)
  • Mountain climbers (15-20 reps)
  • Deep breathing exercises

Equipment Recommendations

  • Resistance bands
  • Dumbbells or kettlebells
  • A yoga mat
